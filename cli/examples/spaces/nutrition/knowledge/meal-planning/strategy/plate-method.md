# The Plate Method

A simple, no-counting approach to balanced meals. Divide your plate visually:

## The Template
- **1/2 plate — Non-starchy vegetables** — Leafy greens, broccoli, peppers, tomatoes, zucchini, cauliflower. Volume and fiber for satiety.
- **1/4 plate — Lean protein** — Chicken, fish, tofu, eggs, legumes. Palm-sized portion (~20–30 g protein).
- **1/4 plate — Complex carbs** — Brown rice, sweet potato, quinoa, whole-grain bread. Fist-sized portion.
- **Thumb of healthy fat** — Olive oil drizzle, avocado slice, handful of nuts.

## Benefits
- No calorie counting or food scales required
- Works at restaurants and social meals
- Naturally portion-controlled (~500–600 kcal per plate)
- Easy to teach to families and children

## Adjustments
- **Weight loss** — Increase vegetables to 2/3, reduce carb portion
- **Athletes** — Increase carb and protein portions proportionally
- **Breakfast** — Same principle: eggs (protein) + oats (carb) + berries (veg/fruit) + nut butter (fat)
