# Batch Prep

Dedicate 2–3 hours once or twice a week to prepare components that mix-and-match into complete meals.

## Core Components to Prep
1. **2–3 proteins** — Grill chicken, bake salmon, cook a pot of lentils
2. **2–3 grains/starches** — Rice, quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes
3. **3–4 vegetables** — Roast a sheet pan of mixed veg, prep raw salad ingredients, steam broccoli
4. **Sauces/dressings** — Tahini dressing, chimichurri, simple vinaigrette

## Storage Guidelines
- **Fridge** — Cooked proteins and grains last 3–4 days. Store in airtight containers.
- **Freezer** — Soups, stews, and cooked grains freeze well for 2–3 months. Portion into single servings.
- **Fresh components** — Wash and chop vegetables, but dress salads only when serving.

## Weekly Template
| Day | Lunch | Dinner |
|-----|-------|--------|
| Mon | Chicken + quinoa + roasted veg | Salmon + rice + steamed broccoli |
| Tue | Lentil bowl + sweet potato | Chicken stir-fry + rice |
| Wed | Salmon salad + quinoa | Lentil soup (from freezer) |
| Thu | Chicken wrap + raw veg | Fresh cook night |
| Fri | Leftovers bowl | Flexible / eat out |

## Tips
- Invest in quality glass containers (different sizes)
- Label everything with date
- Prep while something is in the oven — multitask
