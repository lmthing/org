# Vegetarian Cooking

Vegetarian cooking excludes meat and fish but includes dairy, eggs, and honey.

## Substitution Strategies
- **Protein sources** — Eggs, cheese, legumes (lentils, chickpeas, black beans), tofu, tempeh, seitan, nuts
- **Umami replacements** — Soy sauce, miso paste, nutritional yeast, aged cheeses, mushrooms, tomato paste
- **Stock** — Use vegetable stock or mushroom stock instead of chicken/beef stock
- **Gelatin** — Replace with agar-agar (1:1 ratio, but boil to activate)

## Tips
- Roast vegetables at high heat (220°C+) for caramelization and depth
- Use smoked paprika, liquid smoke, or charred vegetables for smoky notes usually provided by meat
- Don't treat vegetarian food as "meat minus the meat" — build dishes around vegetables as the star
