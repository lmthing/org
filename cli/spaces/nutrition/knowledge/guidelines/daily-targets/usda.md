# USDA Dietary Guidelines (2020-2025)

## Daily Calorie Targets (adults)
- Sedentary: 1,800-2,200 kcal
- Moderately active: 2,000-2,600 kcal
- Active: 2,400-3,000 kcal

## Macronutrient Ranges
- **Protein**: 10-35% of calories (50-175g on 2,000 kcal)
- **Carbohydrates**: 45-65% of calories (225-325g on 2,000 kcal)
- **Fat**: 20-35% of calories (44-78g on 2,000 kcal)
- **Fiber**: 25-30g minimum
- **Added sugars**: <10% of calories (<50g)
- **Sodium**: <2,300mg

## Key Nutrient Targets
| Nutrient | Daily Value |
|----------|-------------|
| Vitamin A | 900 mcg |
| Vitamin C | 90 mg |
| Vitamin D | 20 mcg |
| Calcium | 1,300 mg |
| Iron | 18 mg |
| Potassium | 4,700 mg |

## Food Group Servings (2,000 kcal)
- Fruits: 2 cups
- Vegetables: 2.5 cups
- Grains: 6 oz (half whole grains)
- Protein: 5.5 oz
- Dairy: 3 cups
