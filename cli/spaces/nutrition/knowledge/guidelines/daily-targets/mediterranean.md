# Mediterranean Diet Guidelines

## Core Principles
- Abundant plant foods (fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, nuts)
- Olive oil as primary fat source
- Moderate fish and poultry
- Limited red meat (few times per month)
- Optional moderate red wine

## Macronutrient Targets
- **Protein**: 15-20% of calories
- **Carbohydrates**: 40-50% of calories (complex carbs, whole grains)
- **Fat**: 35-40% of calories (primarily monounsaturated from olive oil)
- **Fiber**: 30-40g (higher than USDA due to legume emphasis)
- **Omega-3**: 1-2g from fish (2-3 servings/week)

## Daily Servings
- Olive oil: 3-4 tablespoons
- Vegetables: 3+ cups (variety of colors)
- Fruits: 2-3 servings
- Whole grains: 3-6 servings
- Legumes: 3+ servings per week
- Fish: 2-3 servings per week
- Nuts/seeds: 1 handful daily
- Red meat: 2-3 servings per month

## Foods to Minimize
- Processed meats
- Refined grains and sugars
- Butter (use olive oil instead)
- Sweetened beverages
