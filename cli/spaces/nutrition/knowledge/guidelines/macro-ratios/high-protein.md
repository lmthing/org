# High Protein Distribution

## Ratio: 40/30/30 (Protein/Carbs/Fat)

Optimized for muscle building, recovery, and satiety.

| Macro | % of Calories | Grams (2,500 kcal) |
|-------|---------------|---------------------|
| Protein | 40% | 250g |
| Carbohydrates | 30% | 188g |
| Fat | 30% | 83g |

## When to Use
- Strength training (4-6x/week)
- Muscle building phases
- Recovery from injury
- High satiety needs

## Protein Timing
- Pre-workout: 20-30g protein, 1-2 hours before
- Post-workout: 30-40g protein within 1 hour
- Distribute remaining evenly across meals
- Casein or slow-digesting protein before bed
