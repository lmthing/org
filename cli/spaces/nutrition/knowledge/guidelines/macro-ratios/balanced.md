# Balanced Macro Distribution

## Ratio: 30/40/30 (Protein/Carbs/Fat)

A moderate, sustainable distribution suitable for general health and moderate activity.

| Macro | % of Calories | Grams (2,000 kcal) |
|-------|---------------|---------------------|
| Protein | 30% | 150g |
| Carbohydrates | 40% | 200g |
| Fat | 30% | 67g |

## When to Use
- General health maintenance
- Moderate exercise (3-4x/week)
- Weight maintenance
- No specific dietary goals

## Meal Distribution
- Breakfast: 25% of daily intake
- Lunch: 35% of daily intake
- Dinner: 30% of daily intake
- Snacks: 10% of daily intake
